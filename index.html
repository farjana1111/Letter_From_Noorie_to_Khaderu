Mera Betaa Khadera

Ketta Bada hogaya mera beta 🥰 ki aaj kaam kar ke khud kama raha hai. Kisi ki nazar na lage tujh 🧿. Tu kab yetta bada hogaya mera bacha Bohot khush hu ki tu tera paav pe khada 🥰,Tere abbu ka sahara pehle bhi tha, ab abi jyda himmat aagai tere abbu mei.
Bas tu tera khayal rakh beta 😘 , kamane ke ismei padko tabiyat kharab karle nakko Tu acha hai tho he tere abbu himmat, teri tabiyat khrab hui tho akele padjati inu. Bas tu dekke jattan se soch samjh kar kadam daal.Ab tu bada hogaya duniya dari dekha hata lekin insana alag hati, tu kamate khot tera dushman bhi tujh sarata..kisi ke baata mei aa nakko.
Mujh se milne nai aya katho dard pad nakko...Tu khush hai so dekke my bohot khush hu. Teri tarakki dekh ke mujh naaz hai mere bete pe 😍...Tu jo bhi karta "My hu tere peeche" 💕. Zindagi mei kuch bhi ho darna mat himmat se kaam le..mera beta sher . Noori ka beta sher 🦁 ne kabhi nhi dil udaas karle nakko my hu tere saath har hamesha.
